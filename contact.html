Common questions
How do sessions work?
All sessions are conducted virtually via Zoom or Google Meet. You'll share documents via Google Drive and receive edits as tracked changes with detailed comments.
How far in advance should I reach out?
Ideally 6–8 weeks before your application deadline. During peak season (Sept–Dec) spots fill quickly, so the earlier the better.
What if I'm not sure which service I need?
That's what the planning call is for! Select "Not sure yet" in the form and Morgann will help you figure out the best approach for your timeline and goals.
Do you work with all types of programs?
Yes — law school, MBA, MPH, MSW, MPA, graduate programs, and more. If you're writing a personal statement or need a resume, Morgann can help.
How does payment work?
Payment is due before services begin. Morgann accepts Venmo, PayPal, and Zelle. Packages can be discussed on a case-by-case basis.
Can I add services after I've started?
Absolutely. Additional editing rounds can be added to any package at $85/round, and individual services can be added as needed.
